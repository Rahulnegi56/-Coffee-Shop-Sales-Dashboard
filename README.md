# ☕ Coffee Shop Data cleaning & Sales Analysis

A retail sales analytics project built to analyze coffee shop performance
across multiple locations and derive actionable business insights.

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze 6 months of transactional sales data across 3 store locations
to identify trends, peak hours, top products, and revenue opportunities.

## 📊 Key Metrics

Metric | Value 
Total Revenue | $6,98,812.33
Total Footfall | 1,49,116
Avg. Bill per Person | $4.69
Avg. Order Value | $1.44

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Hell's Kitchen** is the top performing location with $2,36,511 in sales
- **Barista Espresso** is the best-selling product at $91,406 in revenue
- **Coffee** accounts for 39% of all category sales
- Peak hours are between **8 AM – 10 AM**
- **Friday** is the busiest day with 21,701 orders
